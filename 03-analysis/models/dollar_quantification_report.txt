==============================================================================
全球过度投资估计
==============================================================================

重要方法论说明:
  区域级 MUQ (= delta_GDP_region / GFCF_region) 系统性 < 1，
  因为区域 GFCF 产生跨区域溢出效应 (空间乘数)。
  全球中位区域 MUQ 仅 0.16，这不是"过度投资"，而是测量伪迹。
  因此全球估计使用国家级 WB GFCF 数据 + four_country panel。

--- 国际对比: 投资效率 (WB 口径) ---

注意: WB 口径 MUQ = delta_GDP(current USD) / GFCF(t-1)
这与 Urban Q 框架的 MUQ 口径不同 (Urban Q 使用城镇价值/资本)。
WB 口径 MUQ 系统性 < 1 (因为 GFCF 含折旧替换)，不能直接用于 excess 计算。
此处仅展示趋势对比，说明中国投资效率的相对下降。

--- 关键发现 ---
中国是唯一一个 Urban Q MUQ 从 >1 跌至 <0 的国家，
其投资效率下降幅度远超其他经济体。
其他国家虽然 WB 口径 MUQ 也 <1，但这是折旧效应，
并非本文讨论的"相变"式效率崩溃。

中国 GFCF 全球占比 (2023): ~24%
中国 excess investment (方法 A): US$17.8 tn
占中国 2021-2024 GFCF 的: 21%

--- 规模对照 ---
中国 excess investment (US$17.8 tn) 相当于:
  - 日本 GDP 的 4.2 倍
  - 英国 GDP 的 5.3 倍
  - 全球年度 GFCF 的 0.7 倍

--- 英文版 (Nature 主刊) ---

  "Between 2000 and 2024, we estimate that China's below-parity
   urban investment totalled US$17.8 trillion
   (90% CI: 11.7-24.4 trillion; 120 trillion yuan),
   calculated as the sum of annual FAI weighted by the
   shortfall fraction max(0, 1 - MUQ(t)). This represents
   investment made at a point where each additional yuan
   generated less than one yuan of marginal urban value.
   
   The bulk of excess investment (91%) concentrated in
   2021-2024, coinciding with the phase transition from
   Q > 1 to Q < 1 (Fig. X). During the structural period
   (2000-2020), below-parity investment was US$1.6 trillion,
   driven by episodic MUQ dips rather than persistent overbuilding.
   
   To put this in perspective, China's cumulative below-parity
   investment exceeds the entire GDP of Japan (4.2x)
   and represents 68% of global annual gross
   fixed capital formation."

--- 中文版 ---

  "2000-2024 年间，中国低于边际平价的城镇投资累计约
   120 万亿元 (US$17.8 万亿美元,
   90% CI: 11.7-24.4 万亿美元)。

--- Extended Data 注释 ---

  Methodological note: The excess investment measure is calculated
  as Sum_t[FAI(t) x max(0, 1 - MUQ(t))], where MUQ is the
  weighted marginal urban Q across seven calibrations
  (weights: V1=40%, V2=20%, V3=40%). This proportional method
  corrects the earlier approach of counting 100% of FAI in
  below-parity years, which overestimated excess investment by
  a factor of 2.2x. Monte Carlo uncertainty propagation
  (N=10,000) incorporates weight perturbation (Dirichlet prior),
  MUQ measurement error (sigma=5%), and exchange rate uncertainty (sigma=1.5%).
